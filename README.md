# 7-Lens Operating System  
A structured framework for diagnosing, rebuilding, and scaling service-based businesses

---

## What this is

The 7-Lens Operating System is a practical framework designed to break down how a business actually operates beneath the surface.

It provides a structured way to identify bottlenecks, clarify decision-making, and rebuild operations into scalable, systemised workflows.

Built for real-world application across service-based industries including hospitality, facilities management, and trade businesses.

---

## The Problem

Most businesses don’t struggle because of effort.  
They struggle because of **lack of structure and visibility**.

- Workflows are inconsistent  
- Decisions are reactive  
- Systems are disconnected  
- Growth increases complexity instead of efficiency  

This system exists to create clarity before optimisation.

---

## The 7 Lenses

Each business is analysed through seven core components:

1. **Business Model** — how the business actually generates revenue  
2. **Customer Journey** — how leads convert into paying clients  
3. **Operations** — how work is delivered day-to-day  
4. **Tools & Systems** — what supports or slows the business  
5. **Decision Points** — where human judgement is required  
6. **Exceptions & Risk** — where things break or fail  
7. **Success Metrics** — how performance is measured  

---

## How it works

1. **Input** — structured data capture about the business  
2. **Analysis** — breakdown across the 7 lenses  
3. **Synthesis** — identify constraints and opportunities  
4. **Design** — restructure into scalable workflows  
5. **Build** — implement systems and automation  
6. **Review** — refine based on real-world performance
